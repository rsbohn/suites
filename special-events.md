# Special Events (not for deploy)

- Firefly surge at dusk: glowing dots drifting over the field with a “Firefly watch” note.
- Meteor night: streaking meteors across the sky; characters wish on them.
- Fog morning: low-lying fog rolling over soil and pond; muted palette and dew.
- Eclipse hour: dimmed sky with a moving crescent shadow; timed banner.
- Seed shower: gust-driven drifting seeds; plant shows a temporary growth spurt.
- Lantern walk: warm lanterns along the fence line; gentle flicker.
- Flooded field: waterline rising, objects half-submerged; frogs/birds react.
- Bloom day: plant bursts multiple flowers for a short window.
- Aurora night: subtle ribbons across the sky; quieter creature visits.
- Tool audit day: objects rearranged or lost-and-found; Rae grumbles, Noor muses.
